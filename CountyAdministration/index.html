The Montgomery County LEPC is currently made up of twenty regular members representing the general community, industry, emergency response agencies, county officials, and media personnel. The LEPC is currently divided into four subcommittees that research and address specific areas of the LEPC’s activities and goals. The Montgomery County LEPC meets on a monthly basis at various locations within the district. The public is welcome and encouraged to attend all LEPC meetings to learn of the hazards within their community and the contingency plans in place to protect them from these hazards. A list of the scheduled meetings for 2013 can be found on the schedule page.
